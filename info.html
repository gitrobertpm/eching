e Ching

A free digital simulation of the preeminent ancient form of divination (fortune telling).

The I Ching is thousands of years old.

Its true origins are someting of a mystery.

By methods of summoning the connectedness of all things and the underlying patterns that produce appearant randomness, the I Ching's wisdom, and the universe's will, may be ascertained.

Easy and fun to use.
